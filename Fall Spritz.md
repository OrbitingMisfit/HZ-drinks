# Ingredients
- 0.5 Oz Aperol
- 1.0 Oz Apple Cider
- 2.0 Oz Wycliff
- Club soda



# Instructions
1. Build in wine Glass
2. Add Aperol and Apple cider to glass
3. Fill with Ice
4. add Wycliff
5. Top off with club soda
6. Garnish Orange twist
