# Ingredients
- 1.5 Oz [[Bacardi]] Rum
- 0.5 Oz Pineapple Rum
- 0.5 Oz Lime Juice
- 0.5 Oz [[Simple Syrup]]
- 2.0 Oz Pineapple Juice
- 5-6 dashes Peychaud bitters




# Instructions
1. Build in shaker
2. Shake
3. Pour into double Rocks
4. Top ice
5. Garnish Red cherry
