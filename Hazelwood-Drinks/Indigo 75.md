# Ingredients
- 1.5 Oz [[Morph Gin]] #Needs_Update
- 0.75 Oz [[Simple Syrup]]
- 0.75 [[Lemon Juice]]
- Wycliff

# Instructions
1. Build in shaker (Except Wycliff)
2. Shake 
3. Strain into Coupe
4. Top with Wycliff
5. Garnish with Lemon twist
