# Ingredients
- 1.5 Oz [[Hendriks]]
- 1.0 Oz [[Domaine Canton]]
- 0.5 Oz Lemon juice
- 0.5 Oz Grapefruit juice
- 0.5 Oz [[Rosemary Simple Syrup]]



# Instructions
1. Build in shaker
2. Shake
3. Pour into glass
4. Top with ice
5. Garnish with rosemary sprig
