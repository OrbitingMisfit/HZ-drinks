# Ingredients
- 3 Oz [[Effen Black Cherry]]
- 1 Oz [[Creme de Cacao]]
- 0.5 Oz [[Khalua]]
- Splash Half & Half
- Sprinkle Chocolate Powder

# Instructions
1. Build in Shaker
2. Shake
3. Strain into glass
4. Garnish with cherry kiss and chocolate powder
