# Ingredients
- 1.5 Oz [[Titos]]
- 1.0 Oz [[Pomegranate Juice]]
- 0.5 Oz Lemon juice
- 0.5 Oz [[Agave Simple Syrup]]
- Ginger beer


# Instructions

1. Build in Double Rocks
2. Top with Ice and Ginger beer
3. Garnish with Lemon Wedge
