# Ingredients
- 1.5 Oz [[Jose Cuervo]]
- 0.5 Oz Triple Sec
- 0.75 Oz Lime Juice
- 1.0 Oz Guava syrup



# Instructions
1. Build in shaker
2. Shake
3. Rim glass with Salt
4. Pour into double Rocks
5. Top ice
6. Garnish Lime wheel
