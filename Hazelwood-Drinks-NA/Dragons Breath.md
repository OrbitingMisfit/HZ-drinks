# Ingredients
- 2 Oz [[Strawberry Habanero Simple Syrup]]
- 1.75 Oz Lime juice
- Club soda



# Instructions
1. Build in shaker
2. Shake 
3. Pour into Pint glass
4. Top with club soda
5. Garnish with dehydrated lime
