# Ingredients
- 4 Oz Lemonade
- 0.5 Oz [[Agave Simple Syrup]]
- 0.5 Oz [[Pomegranate Juice]]
- 0.75 Oz Peach Syrup
- Gingerbeer



# Instructions
1. Build in shaker
2. Shake
3. Pour into pint glass
4. Top with Gingerbeer
5. Garnish with lime wheel
