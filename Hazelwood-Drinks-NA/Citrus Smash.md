# Ingredients
- 2 Oz [[Seedlip Grove 42]]
- 0.75 Oz [[Agave Simple Syrup]]
- 0.5 Oz [[Lemon Juice]]
- 1 Oz [[Grapefruit Juice]]

# Instructions
1. Build in Shaker
2. Shake
3. Strain into Double Rocks glass
4. Top with ice
5. Garnish with lemon twist
