# Ingredients
- 0.5 oz Blueberry syrup
- 0.5 oz lemon juice
- 0.5 oz [[Rosmary Simple Syrup]]
- 3 oz soda water
- 3 oz Gingerale



# Instructions
1. Build in reserve wine glass
2. add Blueberry syrup to glass
3. add gingerale and soda water
4. garnish with lemon wheel and rosemary sprig
