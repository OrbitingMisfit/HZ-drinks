# Ingredients
- 1.5 Oz [[Seedlip Garden 108]] #DoubleCheck_Name
- 1 Oz [[Rosmary Simple Syrup]]
- 0.5 Oz [[Lemon Juice]]
- 1.5 Oz [[Grapefruit Juice]]

# Instructions
1. Build in Shaker
2. Shake
3. Pour into Double Rocks
4. Top Ice
5. Garnish with Rosemary or Lemon wedge
