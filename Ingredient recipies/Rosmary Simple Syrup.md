# Ingredients
- 8 cups Water
- 8 cups Sugar
- 10 sprigs Rosemary




# Instructions #review
1. Combine water and rosemary 
2. Bring to a boil 
3. Reduce to simmer
4. Stir in sugar
5. Simmer until sugar is dissolved and light green tint is visible
6. Strain into appropriate cambro (Ensure rosemary sprigs are removed)
