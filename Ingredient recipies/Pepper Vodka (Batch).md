# Ingredients
- 6 Liters Rail Vodka
- 2x Red pepper
- 2x Green pepper
- 3-4 Jalapeno



# Instructions
1. Slice peppers
2. add vodka and peppers to 8 qt cambro
3. Let sit for minimum of 3 days
4. strain into another cambro
5. portion into appropriate container
